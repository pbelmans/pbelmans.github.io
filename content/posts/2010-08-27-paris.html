Apparently, Paris:

  • provides its inhabitants with an amazing metro network, the people using it are very polite too;
  • is the most beige city I ever hope to see, beautiful but beige in every view;
  • contains the wonderful Cité de la Musique with the astounding Musée de la Musique, I hope they will expand their collection (after the pop music extension already that's in the make) with a section devoted to tuning experiments as I'd love to see real life quarter-tone pianos etc. and seeing the UPIC system on which Xenakis composed Mycènes Alpha was a wonderful thing to do apart from the amazing guitar and variants thereupon collection;
  • has other characteristics apart from the ones listed above.
