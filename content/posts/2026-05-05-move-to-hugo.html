Almost ten years ago, I moved from WordPress to Jekyll. It felt like time for another change, so I have now moved the site to Hugo. There was no particular need for it, build times weren't too bad, but it is nice to see how much faster Hugo is in building my website.

The migration is complete, and I have tried to make sure everything still works as intended. Let me know if something did break!
