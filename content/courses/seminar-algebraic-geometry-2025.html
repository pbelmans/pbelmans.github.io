Seminar in Algebraic Geometry

The topic is noncommutative algebraic geometry. First we will discuss some noncommutative projective geometry. Then we will discuss some finite-over-the-center geometry.

The seminar runs Thursday mornings, 11:00–12:45. The location is BBG - 005. In January, we will be in BBG - 077.
