  • Dan Rogalski: Artin-Schelter regular algebras is a survey on AS-regular algebras, the algebras responsible in noncommutative algebraic geometry for noncommutative projective space. A must-read for anyone new to the field.

  • Hiroshi Iritani: Quantum cohomology of blowups and Hiroshi Iritani, Yuki Koto: Quantum cohomology of projective bundles show that the quantum cohomology of a blowup resp. projective bundle behave as one would expect, under the mirror symmetry correspondence and the fact that we know Orlov's blowup formula and projective bundle formula. Really cool!

  • Hodge numbers for smooth Calabi–Yau 3-folds and Topological invariants for known smooth Calabi–Yau 3-folds of Picard number one are two visualisations of numerical data appearing in the classification of Calabi-Yau 3-folds. Really really cool!
