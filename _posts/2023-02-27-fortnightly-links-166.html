  • Daniel Huybrechts: The K3 category of a cubic fourfold – an update is a survey written on the occasion of the Compositio prize which was awarded for the paper The K3 category of a cubic fourfold. Highly recommended if you care about (or wish to be convinced about) understanding cubic fourfolds!

    I can also tell you that there is a little work-in-progress coming up real soon, which was directly inspired by this survey article. Stay tuned!

  • Burt Totaro: Bott vanishing for Fano 3-folds was already discussed in the previous blogpost, but it deserves a spot in the fortnightly links too. Let me in particular advertise the quote

    We can view them as a generalization of toric Fano varieties; they should have some kind of combinatorial classification.

    I look forward to seeing this classification solved!

  • Antonella Grassi, Giulia Gugiatti, Wendelin Lutz, Andrea Petracci: Reflexive polygons and rational elliptic surfaces is an expository and very fun-to-read note on mirror symmetry for del Pezzo surfaces.
