As already announced in an earlier paper announcement post, I have written an Oberwolfach report on the series of joint papers with Sergey Galkin and Swarnava Mukhopadhyay on graph potentials and mirror symmetry for moduli of rank 2 bundles on a curve.

If you are looking for something with more details than a blogpost, but less details than the introductions of the various papers, this might satisfy your needs.
