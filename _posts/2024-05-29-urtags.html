I am really happy to announce URTAGS: the Upper-Rhine and Tributaries Algebraic Geometry Seminar, a joint seminar with the universities of of Basel, Freiburg, Lorraine, Luxembourg, Saarbrücken, Strasbourg, Stuttgart, and Tübingen.

The first edition is scheduled for June 25, and 2 or 3 editions per year (twice in the centrally located Strasbourg, once on location) are planned.

I hope to see some of you there!
