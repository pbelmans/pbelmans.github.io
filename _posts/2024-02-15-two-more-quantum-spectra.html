A little while ago I let my computer waste some electricity on computing the quantum spectra of two exceptional Grassmannians. For more on the quantum spectrum, see this blogpost for more information.

The exceptional Grassmannians are:

  • E7/P3, with Euler characteristic 2016
  • E7/P5, with Euler characteristic 4032
