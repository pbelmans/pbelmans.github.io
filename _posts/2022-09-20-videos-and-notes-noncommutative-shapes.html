Last week we had Noncommutative Shapes, the birthday conference for Michel Van den Bergh. People seemed to have enjoyed the event a lot, so that makes me really happy as an organiser.

I've now posted the recordings to the Noncommutative Shapes YouTube channel, and the notes (whenever available) are also available from the conference website. So even if you had to miss the event, you can still enjoy the great talks that happened last week, I'm really grateful to the speakers for doing such a wonderful job.
