--- layout: post title: I've joined the editorial board of Journal of Noncommutative Geometry type: post categories: - meta author: Pieter Belmans ---

I am pleased and honoured to announce that I have joined the editorial board of Journal of Noncommutative Geometry. The word algebraic is implicit in its name, as the journal serves both the NCG and NCAG communities.

Please submit your interesting and high-quality papers on noncommutative algebraic geometry, so that the journal can continue its role as a leading journal in this particular niche!
