One of the things I do since I started at the University of Luxembourg is help organising the Experimental Mathematics Lab. It is a way of promoting and coordinating anything that can be considered experimental mathematics, and it mostly concerns a series of projects courses in the bachelor's programme, but it also includes bachelor and master theses, and students projects in the master's programme.

At some point we will hopefully also have a physical manifestation of the lab, right now it's purely virtual.

There's a long history, and you can see some of the highlights in the gallery.

All this to say, that:

there is a new website, go check it out!
