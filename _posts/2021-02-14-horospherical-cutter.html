Just a quick service announcement: I have now added Hodge diamonds for horospherical varieties, as discussed in Gonzales--Pech--Perrin--Samokhin, Geometry of horospherical varieties of Picard rank one. Maybe I should spend some time discussing their geometry, but this will be it for now, interested readers are referred to this excellent article.

This is part of an effort to add horospherical varieties to Grassmannian.info, even though they are not quite homogeneous varieties, their geometry is sufficiently close to warrant their inclusion in my opinion.

The usual comment applies: feel free to suggest other varieties whose Hodge diamonds you would like to be implemented!
