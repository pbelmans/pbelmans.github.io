I am really excited to share here the news that I will be moving to Utrecht University, starting from January 1. I am also very much looking forward to integrating in the vibrant Dutch algebraic geometry community, which I participated in as an outsider over the past 10+ years. I will at the same time miss working in Luxembourg.

Today it was also announced that I am the recipient of a Vidi grant, from the NWO, for a project focusing on noncommutative algebraic geometry. I thank the NWO for the confidence they have in me, and I'm looking forward to all of the exciting noncommutative algebraic geometry I will be working on!

I will be announcing (most likely two) PhD positions and a postdoc position at some point, probably to start in September 2025 or thereabouts.
