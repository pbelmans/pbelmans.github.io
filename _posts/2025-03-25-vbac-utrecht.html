Together with Vicky Hoskins and Martijn Kool we are organizing the 2025 edition of VBAC: a conference series with a long-standing tradition. It is a 2-day workshop in Utrecht, May 14 and 15.

There is a website with all information, including the registration form. In the VBAC tradition, we have slots for contributed talks, by younger participants. There is some funding for younger participants too.

I hope to see many of you there!
